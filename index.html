DoodleShip

    Made with:

DoodleShip is a classic arcade game that was released in 1978 and quickly became a cultural phenomenon. The game is set in outer space, where the player controls a small spaceship that must defend the Earth from invading aliens (in this case, small rectangles). The blocks move down your screen, gradually descending towards the player's ship. The player must avoid the blocks. The game is known for its simple but addictive gameplay, and for introducing many of the features that are now considered standard in video games, such as high scores and multiple levels. It has had a significant impact on popular culture, inspiring numerous sequels, spin-offs, and adaptations across various media.
